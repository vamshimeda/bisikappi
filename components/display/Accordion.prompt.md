Accordion for FAQs and grouped detail. Single-open by default; `multiple` allows several. Smooth grid-row height animation.

```jsx
<Accordion
  items={[
    {id:'1', title:'Where are the beans from?', content:'Estates across Chikmagalur & Coorg.'},
    {id:'2', title:'Do you ship?', content:'Yes — across India in 2–4 days.'},
  ]}
  defaultOpen={['1']}
/>
```
