Square, icon-only button for toolbars, cards, and composers. Provide an accessible `label` (also shows as tooltip) and a single icon node as children — it auto-sizes.

```jsx
<IconButton label="Add to cart" variant="solid"><Icon name="plus" /></IconButton>
<IconButton label="Favourite" variant="soft"><Icon name="heart" /></IconButton>
```

Variants: `solid | soft | ghost | outline`. Sizes: `sm | md | lg`.
